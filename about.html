About Us


What Does Our Organisation Do?

GAIA is an organsation that helps to raise awareness to the world of global environmental issues that can become an immediate threat or danger of extinction to ecosystems and animals throughout the world. We offer solutions and possible courses of actions that can be taken by the general public, companies and govermnents.
